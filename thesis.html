Market as public place

Market as public place

Effective space use and accessibility in urban markets is a key element in their success as it directly or indirectly influences the functionality of operation therein. Indra market has continued to experience space use conflict with incidences of people operating in areas not specified and un-organised usage, especially on market paths. This project wishes to address the problem of space use to ensure effective functionality of the market, it intends to devise a model of solving conflict in space use by exploring design and circulation.

Well-designed spaces accrue many benefits such as security, higher economic returns, better legibility and reduced congestion hence better market functionality.

The project made use of both primary and secondary data. It employed use of questionnaires, mapping, photography, observation and conduction of literature review as methods of data collection. The data was analysed by use of Google earth, AutoCAD and MS Excel software after which it was presented in different forms including tables, maps, photos and descriptions.

The project outlines possibilities that can be taken to address the problem, from this, the best alternative is chosen depending on the pros and cons of each alternative as weighed against the desired goals, proposals detailing the best alternative are eventually made. The project concludes with an implementation framework of the proposed market improvement programmes.

Zones

The site is divided into three zones.

zone 1

Acts as magnets to crowds and attracts mainly crowd passing on rajpur road.
Elements- Kiosks, temporary shops, flooring pattern, grand opening to plaza.

zone 2

Provides plaza which can be adapted to different use like for events, functions , flea market.
Play area offers children a safe and recreational place.
Elements - stage, play area, lawn, kiosks, seatings.

zone 3

It is a multi functional area which provides an elevated stage.
It can be used as a private activity area for shopkeepers and be functioned as an event place, local function, celebration and as a spill area.
Food court act as magnet.
Elements- Stage, Lawn, Food Court.
